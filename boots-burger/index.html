Meet our farmers



  • Acton CSA

    Jackson, MA

    Jeremy Acton runs Community Supported Agriculture farm in Jackson, MA. All the vegetables at Bonne Sante come straight from his organic farm. Become a member of the CSA for fresh veggies on the reg.

  • Roger's Range

    Swurst, NH

    Roger Williams raises all of his animals with a grass fed diet and holds a free range farm in NH. He believes that what the animals eat makes a difference in your own health.

  • Valentino Vineyard

    Fairport, MA

    Since 2005, Mike Valentino and his wife have been producing organic, biodynamic wine. They have recently added a micro-brewery to provide eco alcohol.
